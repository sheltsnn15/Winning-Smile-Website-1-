Our Doctors

Meet Our Doctors

Depending what practice you are going to for your dental service you will be either dealing with Kevin or Grace.
Below contains more information on the pair

Dr. Grace Buckley

Dr Buckley qualified from UCC in 2006.
She went on to work in general practice in Kent in the UK for 2 years before returning to work in West Meath and Dublin for several years,
She has decided to return to her native Cork where she has now joined the Winning SmileTeam.
She has completed numerous restorative dentistry courses over the years and she has a keen interest in cosmetic dentistry.
Dr Buckley has competed a 1 year course in aesthetic dentistry with Chris Orr in London and numerous courses in Composite Bonding.
She is an Invisalign provider (clear braces) and loves nothing better than a smile make over tailored to your needs.
Dr Buckley is also well-knowledged in Endodontics (root canal therapy) in the Academy of Advanced Endodontics.
She takes pride in delivering a comfortable and calming patient experience.

Dr. Kevin O’Brien

Dr. O'Brien graduated in dentistry from University College Cork and after a 4 year spell in London in private practice returned to Cork in 1997 to set up his own general practice in Carrigaline.
After 5 years of building, he opened a second practice in glanmire.
both practices were start up and built up gradually over 4/5 years.
He has been travelling to UK, USA, Europe ,and Asia learning new methods and ways to deliver better patient care and to offer our patients more treatment options.
Having branched out into facial cosmetics in 2007 we opened up a new area of business which has seen huge growth worldwide in last decade.
The dentistry has now branched into facial fillers and botox
We only use Allergan Botox which is the market leader and is manufactured in Ireland, which is an added bonus.
This treatment is mainly concentrated around the mouth area to get rid of smokers lines, flat lips, nasolabial folds and marionette lines at the corner of the mouth.
While Botox takes a few days to see the affects filler is more immediate and can last for up to 9 months.
We use both Allergan's Juvederm products and vivacy stylage as our main filler products and both are leading brands in their fields.
